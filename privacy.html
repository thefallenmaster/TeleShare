Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 6, 2026

Information We Collect

TeleShare operates without requiring user accounts. We only collect the necessary information required to process and host your files. Metadata such as file size, IP address during upload, and upload timestamps may be recorded to prevent abuse.

File Content

If you use the password protection feature, your file is encrypted client-side using AES-GCM before it ever leaves your browser. We do not have the decryption key and cannot view the contents of your encrypted files.

Data Retention

Files are stored according to your specified expiration settings. For files with no expiration, they are retained indefinitely by the storage provider until requested to be removed or due to violation of Terms of Service.
